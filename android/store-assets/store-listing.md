## App Name
NBJ Condenser

## Short Description (80 chars max)
Condense YouTube videos to their essence using AI. Share a video, get the key points.

## Full Description
NBJ Condenser uses AI to cut through the fluff in YouTube videos — giving you the condensed version in a fraction of the time.

Share any YouTube video directly to NBJ Condenser. The app sends it to your configured server, which transcribes the audio, strips the filler, and produces a tight condensed video with only the essential content. When it's ready, the video plays automatically.

**Key Features**

• Condense mode — produces a shortened video retaining only essential content, narrated in your chosen voice and language
• Takeaways mode — extracts key bullet-point takeaways from any video
• Real-time progress — live status updates while your video is being processed
• Configurable aggressiveness — choose how aggressively to cut, from light trim to extreme condensation (up to 90% reduction)
• Multiple output modes — video+audio or transcript only
• Voice and language selection — choose from multiple TTS voices and languages
• Speech speed control — play back condensed audio faster or slower
• Recent jobs — quick access to previously condensed videos
• Configurable server — connect to your own NBJ Condenser server or the public instance

**How It Works**

1. Open any YouTube video
2. Tap Share → NBJ Condenser
3. Choose Condense or Takeaways mode and set your preferences
4. Tap the button and wait — real-time progress keeps you informed
5. Watch or read the condensed result

**Requirements**

• Android 7.0 (API 24) or higher
• Internet connection
• Access to an NBJ Condenser server (public server available at conciser.603apps.net)
• A video player app for playback (VLC, MX Player, etc.)

**Privacy**

NBJ Condenser does not collect personal data. YouTube URLs you share are sent only to your configured server. Crash reports are submitted anonymously via Sentry to help improve the app. See our full privacy policy at https://conciser.603apps.net/privacy
