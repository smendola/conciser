🔒 Step 2: Install a non-Play-Store APK

  1. Download the APK above. When it finishes, tap the download notification.
  2. Allow installs from this browser when Android prompts you (Settings → Security → Install unknown apps).
  3. Tap Install and wait for the success toast.
  4. Launch NBJ Condenser from your home screen or app drawer.
You can disable "Install unknown apps" after NBJ Condenser is installed if you want.

🚀 Step 3: Use the app

This app works with the Android "Share" mechanism
  1. Open any video in the YouTube app
  2. Tap the share icon
  3. Find "NBJ Condenser" in the share targets (it's probably under "More" way at the end of the list)
  4. Share to NBJ Condenser
Just opening the app from the Launcher will look beautiful, but it won't do anything useful.
