Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 19, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the NBJ Condenser Android application ("the App") handles information when you use it.

What the App Does

NBJ Condenser lets you share YouTube video URLs to a condensing server (either the public server at conciser.603apps.net or a server you configure yourself). The server transcribes and summarizes the video, and the App plays back the result.

Information We Collect

  • YouTube URLs you share – sent to your configured server to process the video. Not stored by the App itself.
  • Server URL setting – stored locally on your device in app preferences. Never transmitted to us.
  • Output mode, voice, and language preferences – stored locally on your device. Never transmitted to us.
  • Crash reports – if the App crashes, an anonymized crash report may be sent to Sentry (sentry.io) to help us diagnose bugs. Reports include device model, Android version, and a stack trace. They do not include your name, email, or YouTube URLs.

Information We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect your name, email address, or any account information.
  • We do not track your location.
  • We do not access your contacts, camera, microphone, or files.
  • We do not serve advertisements.
  • We do not sell or share any data with third parties, except as described above (crash reporting via Sentry).

Third-Party Services

Sentry – used for crash reporting. See Sentry's Privacy Policy.

Your configured server – YouTube URLs you share are sent to the server URL you configure in the App's settings. If you use the default public server (conciser.603apps.net), that server processes the video and stores the result temporarily. If you run your own server, its data handling is entirely under your control.

Data Retention

The App stores only your settings (server URL and preferences) locally on your device. You can delete them at any time by clearing the App's data or uninstalling it.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The updated version will be posted at this URL with the revised date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue at the project's GitHub repository.
